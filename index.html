01 / Mechanical

MOTOR & ELECTRICAL

If your system vibrates heavily, leaks, or fails to start, the booster pump or SMPS adapter is failing. We diagnose electrical faults to avoid unnecessary part changes and install high-pressure 100/150 GPD original copper-wind motors.

  • Booster Pump Replacement
  • SMPS Power Supply Fixes
  • Auto-Cut & Float Switch Repair

02 / Filtration

MEMBRANE & TDS

Water tasting bitter or flow becoming extremely slow? The RO membrane is clogged. We test raw water TDS and install the correct 75/80 GPD membrane. We replace clogged sediment and activated carbon filters to restore absolute sweetness.
