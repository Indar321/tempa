FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How often should I change my RO filters? ▼

For optimal performance and health safety, pre-filters should be changed every 3-4 months. Inline carbon and sediment filters every 6-8 months, and the main RO membrane every 1.5 to 2 years depending on the inlet water TDS levels in Dantewada.

Do you service brands other than Aayu Water? ▼

Yes! Our expert technicians are trained to service, repair, and maintain all major RO brands. We provide universal spare parts and membranes that fit standard housings.

What is TDS and why does it matter? ▼

TDS stands for Total Dissolved Solids. It represents the total concentration of dissolved substances in water (minerals, salts, metals). High TDS indicates poor water quality. Our systems reduce high TDS to safe, palatable levels while retaining essential minerals via MTDS controllers.

How quickly can I get a technician for repair? ▼

We guarantee a response time of 24 hours for standard service requests. For our Gold and Platinum AMC members, we provide priority 12-hour resolution windows.
