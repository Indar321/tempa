PRIVACY POLICY

DATA COLLECTION AND USAGE PROTOCOLS.

01. Information Collection

Digital Trade collects only the data necessary to fulfill project requirements and communicate effectively. This includes transmission details (name, email, phone) provided via our contact protocols.

02. Data Utilization

All transmitted data is utilized strictly for project development, structural analysis, and client communication. We do not sell, rent, or lease your data to third-party nodes.

03. Security Protocols

We employ industry-standard security measures to protect your digital assets and personal information during the architecture and deployment phases.

TERMS OF SERVICE

STRUCTURAL AGREEMENTS AND OBLIGATIONS.

01. Project Scope

All projects commence only after a formal agreement on the structural scope, timeline, and financial parameters. Any feature creep beyond the agreed scope will require secondary authorization and budgeting.

02. Intellectual Property

Upon final deployment and clearing of all financial invoices, full intellectual property rights of the custom designs and codebases are transferred to the client.

03. Client Responsibilities

Clients are required to provide necessary raw data, brand assets, and feedback within agreed timeframes to ensure project SLA metrics are met.

REFUND POLICY

FINANCIAL RECOVERY PROTOCOLS.

01. Initial Deposits

All structural builds require a non-refundable upfront deposit to secure bandwidth on our servers and initiate the discovery phase.

02. Mid-Project Cancellations

If a project is terminated by the client before handover, billing will be calculated based on the architectural milestones completed up to the point of termination.

03. Post-Deployment

Due to the digital and intellectual nature of our deliverables, we cannot issue refunds once source files have been transferred or code has been pushed live to production servers.
