Knowledge Base

Frequently Asked Questions

How often do I really need to change the filters?

For typical city water, the outer spun (pre-filter) should be changed every 3-4 months. Internal sediment and carbon blocks last 6-8 months. The main RO membrane typically lasts 1.5 to 2 years, depending on input TDS.

Do you service out-of-warranty Aquaguard or Kent systems?

Absolutely. We are brand-agnostic and carry universal, high-grade certified components that perfectly fit and often outperform standard manufacturer replacements in out-of-warranty machines.

Is my water wasting too much through the reject pipe?

Standard RO systems reject about 60-70% of water. If you notice an unusually high rejection rate, it could mean your membrane is choked or the flow restrictor is malfunctioning. We can test and optimize this during a service visit.
