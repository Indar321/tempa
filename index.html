Service 01

Motor & Electrical Diagnostics

If your RO is making strange noises, vibrating heavily, or not turning on, the booster pump or SMPS adapter is likely failing. We diagnose electrical faults precisely to avoid unnecessary part changes.

  • SMPS Power Adapter Replacement
