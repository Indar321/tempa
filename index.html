The Architect

Constructing digital dominance.

At DIGITAL TRADE, we don't just create images; we build structural visual systems. Founded by Indrajeet Pisda and headquartered in Dantewada, our studio bridges the gap between raw creativity and engineered functionality.

We reject the notion that design should be purely aesthetic. True design is an engineered solution to a structural business problem. Whether building a complex Shopify ecosystem or drafting corporate identity, our methodology remains identical: aggressive precision.

Clutter kills conversion. We strip away the unnecessary to deploy high-performance websites and graphics that actually drive your brand forward.

Standard Turnaround Time? Brand identities and logo systems typically require 1-2 weeks. Full-scale web platforms and e-commerce builds take between 3-6 weeks depending on architectural complexity.

What platforms do you build on? We engineer custom front-end solutions. For e-commerce, we specialize in Shopify. For simpler content sites, we utilize platforms like Blogger based on client budget.
